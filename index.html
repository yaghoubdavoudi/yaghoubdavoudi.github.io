About

I am a literary researcher, translator, and journalist with a background in English Literature, literary criticism, and digital media. My work explores the intersections of literary theory, psychoanalysis, film studies, and video game studies.

Alongside my academic interests, I have extensive professional experience in translation, journalism, analytical writing, scriptwriting, and digital media production. I am particularly interested in making complex literary and cultural ideas accessible to broader audiences through writing and educational media.
